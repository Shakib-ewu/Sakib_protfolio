This study addresses the rising road accidents in Bangladesh, highlighting factors such as low traffic awareness and reckless driving. The proposed system uses eye-blink and ultrasonic sensors to control vehicle speed and prevent collisions—detecting driver drowsiness and monitoring obstacles in real time. While effective in improving highway safety, the system currently lacks location tracking and emergency alert features, which are identified for future enhancement.
